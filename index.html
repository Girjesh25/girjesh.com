  2. F.A.Q.
1. How can we contact CM Helpline ?

Ans. Dial 181 (tollfree number) to contact CM Helpline.


2. Is there any time limit to contact CM Helpline ?

Ans. You can call between 7am to 11pm to contact CM Helipline.


3. What type of information can we get from CM Helpline ?

Ans. From CM Helpline you can gather complete information related to MP State Government Schemes.


4. What type of complaints can be registered in CM Helpline ?

Ans. You can register any complaints which are related to MP State Government. However you can register Demands and Suggessions as well.


5. Does CM Helpline provide any other facility/information ?

Ans. Yes, on CM Helpline you can gather Emergency facilities like 108,102,100, Janani Express, etc.
